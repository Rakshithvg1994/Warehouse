Key Takeaways

Current State: Warehouse department is at emerging level (3% overall maturity) across CarlEx Foundations, with particular gaps in zone management standardization, performance management system deployment, and organizational capability development.

Top 3 Priority Areas:

  1. Zone & Workflow Standardization: Create warehouse zone map with ownership, standardize critical workflows, update and implement SOPs
  2. Performance Management System: Deploy cascaded KPIs, establish structured PMS meetings, implement problem-solving discipline
  3. Organizational Capability: Develop skill matrices, establish structured training, build leadership capabilities in CarlEx methodology

Next Steps: Develop detailed Tactical Implementation Plan (TIP) with assigned owners and timelines for each priority area.
